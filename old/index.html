Our Work

Redfieldlane Consulting services the needs of western-centric businesses who wish to explore the business opportunities that Asia has to offer.

Redfieldlane Consulting predominantly targets medium-sized western companies wanting to distribute and sell their products and services into Asia. Many such companies generally cannot afford to set up their own marketing and support and rely on outsourcing until such stage as they can justify the use of their own resources.

Many Western companies are realizing that in order for them to grow their businesses, they will have to look at opportunities in Asia.

Research conducted by Redfieldlane Consulting showed that eight out of ten western businesses have no idea how to go about entering the Asian market.

That is where Redfieldlane Consulting comes in.

Due to extensive Asian experience, Redfieldlane Consulting has the knowledge and contacts to quickly assess the market on behalf of clients and make recommendations as to how the market could be reached.

Due to the vastness of the Asian market and the extensive growth rate in most countries in Asia, many western companies are already trying to get into Asia, or at least considering it.

Redfieldlane Consulting believes that it has what it takes to assist companies to do just that.

Redfieldlane Consulting has a footprint in Asia, previous Asian experience, technology know-how and qualified personnel.
